V3.1 — Improvements

V3.1 fixes the back and edges:

  • Thin back (1/4 width): Back depth reduced from 0.4 to 0.1 units — much thinner, less bulky
  • Same color front/back: Back vertices now use the SAME colors as front (was 70% darker before)
  • Smoothed edges: Laplacian smoothing applied (2 iterations) — softens jagged left/right/back edges to match the front
  • More zoomable: Camera range expanded — zoom in to 1m (was 3m), zoom out to 50m (was 25m)

Technical Specs

  • Grid: 800×800 = 640,000 candidate vertices
  • All 4 models: 10MB+ (13-21 MB each)
  • Source: 4096×4096 (4K, transparent BG)
  • Relief depth: 0.9 units (front), back thickness: 0.1 units (1/4 of original)
  • Smoothing: Laplacian, 2 iterations, lambda=0.3
  • Watertight (manifold) — 3D-printable
